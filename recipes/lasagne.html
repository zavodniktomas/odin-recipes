Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Parmesan cheese

Red Sauce

  • Carrot
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Minced beef
  • Red Wine
  • Tomato Paste
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Thyme
  • Tomato puree
  • Beef broth
  • Sugar

White Sauce

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Red Sauce

  1. Chop carrot, celery and onion.
  2. Sear the vegetable on olive oil and then add minced beef meat. Sear until meat is brown.
  3. Add red wine, tomato paste, salt, pepper, thyme and tomato puree.
  4. Add beef broth and little bit of sugar and simmer for 3 hours.

White Sauce

  1. Add butter on pan and let it melt.
  2. Mix flour into the melted butter.
  3. Add hot milk.

Layering

  1. First layer is white sauce, red sauce and then sheets of lasagne.
  2. Additional layers are red sauce white sauce and sheets of lasagne
  3. Last layer is topped with parmesan cheese
